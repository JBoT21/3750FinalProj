Web App Proposal:

Introduction

This proposal outlines the development of a web application for our final project that aims to solve specific problems faced by users. The application will be designed with user experience in mind, ensuring that it is intuitive and easy to navigate. It is a demonstration of our skills that we learned in the course of CPSC 3750: Web App Development.

Objective

The objective of this web application is to design a simple web app that combines the skills that we learned over the course of the semester that is functional, efficient, and easy to use. The app will be a simple store that makes use of a database to store different items and their prices. It will be called Golfly (pronounced as: Golf-LEE) and will be a simple store for all your golfing needs!

Features

The app will enable users to add and remove items from their cart, view their selections, and see the total cost in real time. It will also provide functionality for completing purchases. On the backend, the app will interact with a database to update inventory based on purchases or restocks.

(Extra Feature): The app will also be capable of making API calls to a golf course data provider, allowing users to browse golf courses and view their pricing information.

Timeline

The project will be completed in the following phases:

Phase 1: Project Planning and Requirements Gathering

Phase 2: Designing, Researching, and Getting the Thing to Work

Phase 3: Implementing the Frontend and Wrapping it up
